All Saints’ Day

All Saints’ Day (November 1) is a Christian celebration that honors all the saints, known and unknown, who have lived holy lives and now share eternal life with God. It is a day to remember their faith, sacrifices, and examples of holiness that inspire believers. In the Philippines, families often visit cemeteries to clean and decorate the graves of loved ones, showing respect and offering prayers. It is both a religious and cultural occasion marked by togetherness and remembrance.
