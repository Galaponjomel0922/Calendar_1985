All Souls’ Day

All Souls’ Day (November 2) is dedicated to praying for the souls of the faithful departed, especially those believed to be in purgatory. Catholics offer masses, prayers, and candles for their loved ones, asking God for their eternal rest. In many cultures, including the Philippines, families gather in cemeteries to spend time in prayer and remembrance. This day emphasizes love, hope, and the belief in eternal life after death.
