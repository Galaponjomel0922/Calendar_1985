Easter Sunday

Easter Day is the most important celebration in Christianity, commemorating the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead after His crucifixion. It is a joyful day that symbolizes victory over sin and death, bringing hope and renewal to believers. In the Philippines and many other countries, Easter is celebrated with the “Salubong” at dawn, joyful masses, and festive gatherings. It marks the end of Holy Week and the beginning of the Easter season in the Christian calendar.
