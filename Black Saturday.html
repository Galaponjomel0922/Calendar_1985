Black Saturday

Black Saturday, also known as Holy Saturday, is the day between Good Friday and Easter Sunday that commemorates the time Jesus Christ’s body lay in the tomb after His crucifixion. It is a day of mourning and reflection for Christians, symbolizing the silence and waiting before the celebration of the resurrection. In the Philippines, it is observed with solemnity, with most religious activities paused until the Easter Vigil Mass at night. This vigil marks the transition from sorrow to the joy of Christ’s resurrection on Easter Sunday.
